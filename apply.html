Background Check Required

All renters must pass a criminal background check before rental approval. You have two options:

Option 1: Pay for your own check (recommended)

Click the button below — results go directly to us. ($17, processed by LeaseRunner)

If you prefer, we'll order the report and add the cost to your rental invoice.
